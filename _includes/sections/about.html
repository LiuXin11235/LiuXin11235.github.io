About Me

HI! I am a researcher at Supcon working on enterprise intelligence. Our team aims to build enterprise-grade intelligent systems with hypergraph models to enable steady and controllable business growth, including:

数据智能体 会议智能体 标书智能体 销售智能体 采购智能体 人力智能体 效率派

My research work mainly focuses on using (hyper-)graph, ontology and large language models (LLMs) to encapsulate enterprise knowledge, business entities, and operational logic, enabling structured reasoning and task planning while preserving explainability and execution determinism, with a focus on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) methods and graph foundation models.

Before joining Supcon, I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University in 2025, advised by Prof. Wei Xu, where I worked on fraud detection, multi-party computation, graph algorithms, and graph neural networks. The pursuit of my Ph.D. degree was greatly supported by my advisor, mentors, friends and family ❤️.
